Welcome to "The Wordshift" - How to Play

"The Wordshift" is a thrilling word guessing game that challenges your vocabulary and strategic thinking. Here's how to play:

  1. Getting Started:
    • To begin the game, enter your initials and hit the start button.
    • You'll be presented with a word to guess, consisting of 5 letters.
  2. Guessing Words:
    • Use the provided keyboard interface or your device's keyboard to input your word guesses.
    • Ensure that your guess is a valid English word.
  3. Feedback on Guesses:
    • After each guess, the game provides feedback on the correctness of your letters:
      • Gray Letters: Indicates that the letter is not in the correct word.
      • Yellow Letters: Shows that the letter is in the word but not in the correct position.
      • Green Letters: Confirms that the letter is in the correct position.
  4. Shifting and Removing Words:
    • If you correctly guess a word, all previous guesses will shift up, and the top guess will be removed.
    • The colors of the letters in the guesses will adjust based on the new word to be guessed.
  5. Limited Tries:
    • You have 6 tries initially to guess the word correctly.
    • Once you exhaust your tries, the game ends. But don't worry; you can always start a new game!
  6. Gameplay Frequency:
    • Please note that you can only play "The Wordshift" once every 24 hours.
    • This ensures fairness and allows everyone a chance to enjoy the game.
  7. Sharing Your Score:
    • After the game ends, you can share your score by clicking on the "Copy Link" button.
    • A modal will appear with a shareable link. Simply copy the link and send it to anyone.
  8. Leaderboard:
    • Check out the leaderboard to see where you stand among all-time top scorers. Can you make it to the top?
  9. Help:
    • If you ever need assistance during the game, click on the "Help" button to access this guide.

Now that you know the rules, it's time to put your word skills to the test. Enjoy playing "The Wordshift" and see how many words you can guess correctly!
